Welcome, Sensei!

Schale DB is an unofficial database for the mobile game Blue Archive developed by Nexon Games and published by Yostar (Japan) and Nexon (Global) for iOS and Android.

Please select your game server using the settings menu to see only information that has been released on that server.

Features
  • View Student info, skills and calculate their stats at any level, star grade or equipment tier, including their summon or vehicle.
  • Complete Items, Equipment and Furniture database.
  • Reward drops, maps and detailed enemy information for all Campaign, Event Quest, Challenge and Daily stages.
  • Stats and Skill information for Total Assault, Joint Exercise and Combined Operation raids.
  • View the chances of possible items received from crafting nodes.
